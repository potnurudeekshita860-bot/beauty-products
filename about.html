Our Journey

Six Years of Radiance

2018
The Beginning

Meera Krishnan, a biochemist and beauty enthusiast, begins blending formulas in her Mumbai apartment. The first product — a rosehip face oil — is shared among 20 friends.

2019
First Retail Partner

Lumière launches in 3 premium boutiques across Mumbai and Delhi. The Golden Hour Serum sells out within 48 hours of launch.

2021
Going Digital

Lumière.in launches with full e-commerce. Within 6 months, the brand ships to 30+ cities and acquires its first 10,000 customers.

2023
200,000 Happy Clients

Lumière reaches a milestone of 200,000 customers and launches the Soir Fragrance and Velvet Makeup collections — entering two entirely new beauty categories.

2024
The Future

With a new R&D lab in Pune and partnerships with 12 botanical farms, Lumière enters its most exciting chapter yet. New collections launching every quarter.
