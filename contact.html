FAQ

Common Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 4–7 business days. Express delivery (2–3 days) is available for ₹99. Free standard shipping on all orders above ₹999.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused, unopened products. For products with a quality issue, we accept returns regardless of usage. Full refund processed within 5–7 business days.

Are your products safe for sensitive skin?

All Lumière products are dermatologist-tested and free from parabens, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances. We recommend patch-testing any new product. Products marked "Sensitive" are specifically formulated for sensitive skin types.

Do you offer skincare consultations?

Yes! Our team of trained beauty advisors offer free 20-minute virtual consultations. Book via our website or call our toll-free number. We'll help you build your perfect routine.

Are your products genuinely cruelty-free?

Absolutely. Lumière is PETA-certified cruelty-free and 100% vegan. We never test on animals and none of our suppliers do either. Our certification is renewed annually.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order ships, you'll receive an SMS and email with your tracking number. You can track your order in real-time through our partner courier's website or our order tracking page.
